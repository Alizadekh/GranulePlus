Plastic material recycling codes

The following figure shows the plastic waste recycling symbol as follows.
1- The first step in the recycling symbol represents the collection and washing of waste.
2- The second stage represents the production of recycled granules and the final product.
3- The third stage or the last flash represents the actual purchase and use of new products made from recycled materials.
In each triangle there is a number from 1 to 7, each of which indicates a specific type of recycled plastic. No. 5 indicates polypropylene plastic, which is very hard and light and has excellent heat resistance. Products such as yogurt containers, shampoo bottles, soft drinks, margarine containers and diapers are produced from this plastic.
No. 7 refers to other types of plastics that plastics such as polyamide, ABS, HIPS are in this category. They are used to make objects such as bicycle saddles and industrial parts.
